What is the focal length calculator?
The focal length calculator is a tool that calculates the magnification, focal length, and the angle of view.
How to calculate focal length?
To calculate focal length you should use the following formula:
focal length = (object distance / ((1 / magnification) + 1)) * 1000
Why use focal length calculator?
The focal length calculator is used in order to measure the magnification, focal length, and the angle of view.
